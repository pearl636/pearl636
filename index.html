CP is not my boss

Hi readers, I would first like to welcome, everyone who decides to read this very first post of a rookie writer like me. What do I mean by C.P. is not my boss you may wonder. Let me begin by explaining what C.P. is for people who may not know much about this diagnosis.   C.P. stands for Cerebral Palsy. It can happen during pregnancy or shortly after birth. It is basically an injury to the brain. It can impact individuals differently from mild to serve. It mainly affects a person mobility. However, it can be mixed up with other struggles such as learning challenges. Now, that I  have given an overview. Let's start off with 10 ways C.P. is not my Boss!!! 1.I am active daily going out to places 2. I am a student. 3. I socialize with others who may or may not have a diagnosis. 4. I am not afraid to speak about C.P. when asked by people. 5. I don't keep myself in victim mode of C.P. (switch it off as quickly as possible. 6. cry less smile more! 7. Use it to teach others. 8. Treat it as your style of life (NOT your condition) 9. figure out your personal map and find ways around your obstacles 10. Focus on your strengths  (Not your weakness)
